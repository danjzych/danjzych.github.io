A bit more about me!

I was born in the suburbs of Chicago, where I lived until I moved away to attend the Univeristy of Wisconsin-Madison in 2012. I graduate with a B.S. in Psychology and Philosophy in 2016.

After graduating, I worked in a psychology research lab briefly before working in a domestic abuse shelter I previously volunteered at as a student.

I left the shelter to work at a start-up my friend was working at. I worked there for four years, discovering my love of tech along the way. After working on the product team for four years, I left to work as a touring musician. I have recently finished touring, and am now looking forward to beginning a career as a software developer!

Outside of work, I love to play and produce music, skateboard, eat good food and drink good coffee, and hang out with my partner and our kittens (Okay, fine, they are three years-old, but they're always gonna be kittens, ya know?)
